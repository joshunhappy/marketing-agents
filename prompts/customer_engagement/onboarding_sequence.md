## Sequence Structure (5 emails)

**Email 1 — Day 0 (Welcome)**
- Warm welcome, confirm what they signed up for
- One thing to do right now (the "first win")
- Link to key getting-started resource

**Email 2 — Day 2 (Quick Win)**
- Celebrate if they've taken the first step
- Surface one feature that delivers fast value
- Tip that 80% of new users miss

**Email 3 — Day 5 (Deeper Value)**
- More advanced use case relevant to their profile
- Case study or social proof from similar customer

**Email 4 — Day 10 (Check-in)**
- How are things going? Invite feedback
- FAQs / common friction points addressed
- Link to support resources

**Email 5 — Day 14 (Success Path)**
- Recap of what they've accomplished
- Preview of what's possible next (plant upsell seed)
- Invitation to community or next event

Keep each email under 200 words. Subject lines under 50 characters.
