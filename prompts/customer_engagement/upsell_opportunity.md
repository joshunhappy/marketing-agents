## Email Requirements
- Subject: curiosity or value-led, never "upgrade now"
- Opening: acknowledge their current success / usage — make them feel seen
- Body: present the upgrade as a natural next step, not a sales pitch (< 150 words)
- Specific benefit: one clear, relevant feature they're missing that maps to their usage

- Closing: warm, human sign-off
