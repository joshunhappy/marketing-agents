## Assessment Framework
1. **Health Score**: Green (healthy) | Yellow (at risk) | Red (CHURN RISK)
2. **Engagement Level**: Active | Declining | Dormant
3. **Upsell Readiness**: Is there a clear upsell or cross-sell opportunity? If yes, label as UPSELL
4. **Onboarding Status**: Are they new (< 14 days)? What onboarding support do they need?
5. **Key Signals**: The 2–3 most important data points driving your assessment
6. **Recommended Action**: Specific next step (email, call, in-app message, no action)

Be direct. One sentence per point where possible.
