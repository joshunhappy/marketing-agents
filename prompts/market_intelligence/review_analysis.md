## Your Analysis Should Cover
1. **Top Pain Points** — The most frequently mentioned frustrations (ranked by frequency)
2. **Delight Drivers** — What customers love most (use these in testimonials and messaging)
3. **Unmet Needs** — Requests or gaps that represent upsell or product opportunities
4. **Language Patterns** — Exact phrases customers use to describe their problems (use these verbatim in copy)
5. **Churn Signals** — Review language that predicts churn risk
6. **Key Actions** — 3 specific messaging or product changes suggested by this data

Quantify where possible (e.g., "7 of 20 reviews mention X").
