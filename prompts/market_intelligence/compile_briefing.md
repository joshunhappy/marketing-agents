## Briefing Format
Produce a single document with:
- **This Week's Top 3 Insights** (the most important things the team needs to know)
- **Competitive Landscape** (2–3 bullet summary)
- **Market Trends** (2–3 bullet summary)
- **Customer Signals** (2–3 bullet summary)
- **Recommended Actions This Week** (numbered priority list, max 5 items)
- **Watch List** (things to monitor for next cycle)

Keep the briefing to 400–600 words. Write for a marketing director who has 5 minutes to read it.
