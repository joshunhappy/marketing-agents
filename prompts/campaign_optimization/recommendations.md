## Recommendation Format
For each recommendation provide:
- **Action**: Exactly what to change (specific campaign, ad set, or setting)
- **Rationale**: The data point(s) that support this change
- **Expected Impact**: What metric should improve and by how much
- **Priority**: High (act today) | Medium (this week) | Low (next sprint)
- **Effort**: Low (< 30 min) | Medium (half day) | High (multi-day)

Group recommendations by: Immediate Actions | This Week | Next Cycle

Cap the list at 10 recommendations. Quality over quantity.
