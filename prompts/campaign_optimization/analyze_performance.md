## Analysis Framework
1. **Top Performers** — Campaigns/ad sets with best CTR, conversion rate, and ROAS
2. **Underperformers** — Assets to pause or restructure (flag if spend > $X with 0 conversions)
3. **Trend Analysis** — Performance trajectory over the period (improving, declining, stable)
4. **Anomalies** — Unusual spikes or drops worth investigating
5. **Creative Fatigue Signals** — CTR declining on previously strong creatives
6. **Audience Overlap / Saturation** — Signs of targeting the same people across campaigns

Be specific. Reference actual numbers from the data. Flag anything requiring urgent action.
