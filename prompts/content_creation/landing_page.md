## Structure
Produce copy for each section in order:

1. **Hero** — Headline (8–12 words), subheadline (20–30 words), primary CTA button text
2. **Problem** — 2–3 sentences naming the pain point the visitor feels right now
3. **Solution** — 2–3 sentences introducing our product as the answer
4. **Features / Benefits** — 3 benefit-led bullet points (feature → benefit format)
5. **Social Proof** — 1 testimonial placeholder with ideal quote structure
6. **FAQ** — 3 objection-handling Q&As
7. **Final CTA** — Closing headline + CTA button text + risk-reversal line (e.g., "No credit card required")

Output as structured Markdown. Do not include design instructions — copy only.
