## Brand Audit Checklist
For each content sample found in the outputs, assess:
1. **Tone**: Does it match our brand voice? (professional, approachable, confident)
2. **Prohibited words**: Any use of banned terms (synergy, leverage, disruption, etc.)?
3. **CTA clarity**: Does each piece have a single, clear CTA?
4. **Value framing**: Does it lead with customer benefit, not product features?
5. **Channel fit**: Is the register appropriate for the channel?

## Output Format
- **Overall Brand Consistency Score**: X/10
- **Flagged Issues**: Specific examples with the problematic text quoted
- **Positive Examples**: Copy that perfectly matches brand voice (worth reusing as templates)
- **Recommendations**: Specific prompt or guideline changes to improve consistency

Be specific. Quote actual text when flagging issues.
