## Recommendation Format
Provide 5–7 strategic recommendations, each with:
- **Recommendation**: The specific strategic action
- **Evidence**: What data/signals support this
- **Expected Outcome**: What will improve and roughly by how much
- **Owner**: Which agent or human team member should execute
- **Timeframe**: This week | This month | This quarter

End with a **Quarterly Outlook**: given current trajectory, are we on track to hit our KPIs (+40% leads, +25% conversion, 10x content, -30% CAC)? What is the biggest risk to hitting them?

Be direct and opinionated. "Continue current approach" is not a recommendation.
