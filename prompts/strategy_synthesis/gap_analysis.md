## Analysis Framework
1. **Content-Audience Misalignment**: Is our content reaching the right ICP segments?
2. **Message Inconsistency**: Do competitor insights conflict with what we're publishing?
3. **Funnel Leakage**: Where are leads dropping off? What's upstream causing it?
4. **Channel Conflicts**: Are we over-investing in a channel that campaign data says is underperforming?
5. **Retention Blind Spots**: Are we spending on acquisition while ignoring at-risk customers?
6. **Agent Coverage Gaps**: What is no agent currently monitoring that could hurt us?

For each gap identified: describe the gap, its potential impact, and a recommended fix.
